In the modern era, this medium has even moved onto the online platform for added ease to the reader.

The difference between novel and book is that every novel is a book, but every book is not a novel.

What is Novel?

A novel is a fictional narrative that contains a storyline filled with fictitious characters, plots, dialogues, and ploys. Novelists have been able to use their artistic license to explore the world of imagination and help readers find a new way to express their creativity.
Novels are written on bound pages, and hence they are considered a subset of books. We can also say that all novels are books, just with a different form of information.
Novels are a genre of fiction, and here the novelist helps the reader expand the limits of their creativity with the help of a world created out of pure imagination. So, the real purpose of novels is not only to provide entertainment and pleasure but also to help the reader find a different perspective.
The first novels were written during the 11th century, with the oldest dating back to the late 1000s, where a Japanese noblewoman had written the first novel in Japanese. The first English novels were not written and published until 1485 by Thomas Malory.
Over the millennia, rules and instructions were put in place to classify a book as a novel. Word limits were laid down so that one could distinguish between a novel and short stories. Generally, the minimum word count is in the range of 40,000-50,000. The genre that comes under novels is romantic, mysterious, mythical, historical, gothic, and many more.

What is Book?

A book is a collection of bound pages that encompasses all forms of available written work that is available for a reader. Every century we see some sort of advancement in the way books are written or what new art form that can be invented.
In the modern era, humankind has taken a massive leap with the reading format and has allowed the reader to access books on an online platform through e-books. E-books have given the reader the power of easy accessibility and availability.
The first traces of books or collection of pages can be dated back to even before 2100 BC, and scholars still believe there could be evidence to suggest that books might have existed many millennia before that.
There is no proper word limit or page count while writing books, as it all depends on the margins width, page size and fonts used. Generally, a book comprises of 100+ pages, and the word count varies accordingly. A book can have many categories under it, and they range from textbooks to fiction and nonfiction, and even instruction manuals and guidebooks.

Main Differences Between Novel and Book

  • A novel is a fictional narrative written on book pages.
    Books are a collection of all written works in existence, and hence all novels are books, but all books are not novels.
  • The first novel ever published is dated back to the 11th century in Japan.
    The origin of the first book is yet to be dated, with proof of the existence of manuscripts before 2100 BC.
  • Novels are stories portrayed by the writer with the help of fictitious characters and plotline,
    while books contain any type of information that a writer wishes to put on paper.
  • Novels have a specific minimum word count of 40,000- 50,000,
    while there is no particular instruction but rather a guideline that a book must have 100+ pages.
  • Examples of novels are The Great Gatsby and The Hobbit, which are all fictional storylines,
    while books can range from textbooks to fiction and nonfiction works.

Conclusion

Novels and books both in a way help a writer convey some form of information to the audience and hence are a medium for sharing and connecting.
Novels allow the reader to divulge themselves into a world of fiction and imagination, where their creativity is pushed and explored.

Novels will enable one to connect with a different perspective and help us ease into a creative mindset. Books are a collection of pages,that encompass categories such as manuscripts, fictional and nonfictional works, and are a superset to all written works in existence.
Books give the reader all sorts of information, and there are a vast number of books of various topics. As novels are a category that comes under books, all novels are primarily books,
but all books are not novels.
